# CTA Formulas Reference

Call-to-action templates for the final carousel slide.

## Core CTA Types

### 1. Follow CTA
Best for: growing your audience

**Templates:**
- "Follow @handle for daily [topic] tips"
- "Follow for more [topic] content"
- "Hit Follow so you don't miss Part 2"
- "Follow @handle — I post [topic] content every [day]"

### 2. Save CTA
Best for: educational/reference content

**Templates:**
- "Save this for later — you'll need it"
- "Bookmark this carousel for your next [task]"
- "Save + Share with your team"
- "This took me [X hours] to research. Save it in 1 tap."

### 3. Share CTA
Best for: relatable/inspirational content

**Templates:**
- "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- "Tag a friend who still does [wrong thing]"
- "Send this to your [role] friend"
- "Share if you agree"

### 4. Comment CTA
Best for: engagement/algorithm boost

**Templates:**
- "Which tip was your favorite? Comment below"
- "Drop a [emoji] if you learned something new"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Agree or disagree? Tell me in the comments"
- "Comment '[word]' and I'll send you the full guide"

### 5. Link CTA
Best for: driving traffic

**Templates:**
- "Full guide linked in bio"
- "Get the free template — link in bio"
- "Download the checklist at [link]"
- "Free resource in bio — grab it before I take it down"

## Compound CTAs

Combine 2 actions for maximum engagement:

- "Save this + Follow @handle for more"
- "Share with a friend + Drop a [emoji] below"
- "Comment your favorite + Follow for Part 2"
- "Save for later + Tag someone who needs this"

## Social Proof Elements

Add credibility to your CTA:

- "Join 50K+ followers learning [topic] daily"
- "Trusted by 10,000+ [professionals]"
- "Part of a free [topic] series — follow to catch them all"
- "This post helped 5,000+ people — will you be next?"

## CTA Design Principles

1. **One primary action** — Don't overwhelm with choices
2. **Use a button shape** — Rounded rectangle in accent color signals clickability
3. **Include the handle** — Always show @username for brand recall
4. **Match the energy** — Educational content gets calm CTAs, hype content gets urgent CTAs
5. **Add a reason** — "Follow for X" outperforms bare "Follow me"

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- "Save" and "Share" are most algorithmically valuable
- Use "Send to Stories" to encourage resharing
- Comments boost post visibility in Explore

### LinkedIn
- "Repost" is the primary sharing action
- Engagement comments ("Agree?") drive visibility
- "Follow for more" works well for thought leadership

### Twitter/X
- "Retweet" for sharing
- Thread format: "Follow + turn on notifications"
- Quote tweet prompts drive engagement

## Matching CTA to Content Type

| Content Type | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| Tips/How-to | Save | Follow |
| Motivation | Share | Comment |
| Data/Stats | Save | Share |
| Opinion | Comment | Share |
| Story | Follow | Comment |
| Product | Link | Follow |
| Comparison | Comment | Save |
