# Hook Patterns Reference

20+ proven hook formulas for carousel slide 1. Each formula includes a template and examples.

## Category 1: Listicle Hooks

### 1. Number + Outcome
**Template:** `X [Things] That Will [Outcome]`
- "7 AI Tools That Will Replace Your Designer"
- "5 Habits That Will Double Your Productivity"
- "12 Free Resources Worth $10,000+"

### 2. Number + Curiosity
**Template:** `X [Things] I Wish I Knew [Timeframe]`
- "10 AI Tools I Wish I Knew Sooner"
- "8 Career Mistakes I Wish I Avoided in My 20s"
- "6 Money Rules I Wish I Learned in School"

### 3. Number + Superlative
**Template:** `The X Most [Adjective] [Things] in [Domain]`
- "The 5 Most Underrated Design Tools in 2026"
- "The 10 Most Powerful ChatGPT Prompts"

## Category 2: Question Hooks

### 4. Challenge Question
**Template:** `Are You Still [Doing X Wrong]?`
- "Are You Still Posting at the Wrong Time?"
- "Are You Still Using Excel for This?"

### 5. Curiosity Question
**Template:** `Why Do [Group] Always [Action]?`
- "Why Do Top Designers Always Use Grids?"
- "Why Do Millionaires Wake Up at 5 AM?"

### 6. Self-Assessment
**Template:** `Can You [Do X]? Most People Can't.`
- "Can You Pass This Design Quiz? Most Can't."
- "Can You Name All 10? Most People Get 3."

## Category 3: Contrarian Hooks

### 7. Stop Doing X
**Template:** `Stop [Doing X]. Here's Why.`
- "Stop Using Hashtags. Here's Why."
- "Stop Multitasking. Here's Why."

### 8. Myth Buster
**Template:** `[Common Belief] Is a Myth`
- "Working Hard Guarantees Success Is a Myth"
- "You Need 10K Followers to Make Money Is a Myth"

### 9. Unpopular Opinion
**Template:** `Unpopular Opinion: [Statement]`
- "Unpopular Opinion: College Is a Waste of Time"
- "Unpopular Opinion: Morning Routines Are Overrated"

## Category 4: Authority Hooks

### 10. Expert Reveal
**Template:** `What [Experts] Don't Tell You About [Topic]`
- "What Designers Don't Tell You About Color"
- "What Top CEOs Don't Tell You About Hiring"

### 11. Behind the Scenes
**Template:** `How I [Achievement] in [Timeframe]`
- "How I Gained 100K Followers in 6 Months"
- "How I Built a $1M Business from My Phone"

### 12. Study/Research
**Template:** `I [Studied/Analyzed] X [Things]. Here's What I Found.`
- "I Analyzed 1,000 Viral Posts. Here's What I Found."
- "I Tested 50 AI Tools. Here Are the Top 10."

## Category 5: Urgency Hooks

### 13. Time-Sensitive
**Template:** `[Do X] Before [Timeframe/Event]`
- "Learn These AI Tools Before 2027"
- "Master These Skills Before AI Takes Over"

### 14. Warning
**Template:** `Don't [Action] Until You Read This`
- "Don't Quit Your Job Until You Read This"
- "Don't Buy a House Until You Know These 5 Things"

### 15. Deadline
**Template:** `You Have [Time] to [Action]`
- "You Have 6 Months to Learn AI or Get Left Behind"

## Category 6: Curiosity Gap Hooks

### 16. Secret Reveal
**Template:** `The [Adjective] Secret to [Outcome]`
- "The Lazy Person's Secret to Getting Fit"
- "The Ugly Truth About Social Media Growth"

### 17. Hidden/Unknown
**Template:** `[X] Hidden [Things] You're Missing`
- "7 Hidden iPhone Features You're Missing"
- "5 Hidden Instagram Tricks for More Reach"

### 18. Comparison
**Template:** `[X] vs [Y]: Which One Wins?`
- "ChatGPT vs Claude: Which One Wins?"
- "Remote Work vs Office: The Data Is In"

### 19. Transformation
**Template:** `From [Bad State] to [Good State] in [Timeframe]`
- "From 0 to 10K Followers in 30 Days"
- "From Broke to Six Figures in 1 Year"

### 20. Mistake-Based
**Template:** `X [Mistakes] Killing Your [Goal]`
- "5 Mistakes Killing Your Instagram Growth"
- "7 Resume Mistakes That Get You Rejected"

## Hook Writing Rules

1. **Max 8-10 words** — Every word must earn its place
2. **Use odd numbers** — 7, 9, 13 outperform even numbers
3. **Power words**: secret, proven, mistake, hack, truth, myth, never, always, free, hidden
4. **Avoid**: "Introduction to", "A Guide to", "Everything About"
5. **Test the scroll-stop**: Would YOU stop scrolling for this?
6. **Match the promise**: Hook must accurately preview the content that follows
